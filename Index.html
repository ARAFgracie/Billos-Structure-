03 — ACCESS CONTROL

Three tiers. One rulebook.

TIER 01

Authority

Full control across every shop in the account. Switches between dashboards, manages every role.

TIER 02

Owner

Full control within their own shop — sales, inventory, staff, and reports for that location only.

TIER 03

Sales Executive

Billing access only. No settings, no reports — built for speed at the counter.

Clicking a staff name opens the role dropdown — selecting a new tier updates permissions across the app instantly, no re-login needed.
